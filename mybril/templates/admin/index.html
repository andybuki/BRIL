Open Provenance Model Vocabulary (OPM-V) Notation


Circle Artifact is a general concept that represents immutable piece of state, which may have a physical embodiment in a physical object, or a digital representation in a computer system.
Square Process refers to an action or series of actions performed on or caused by artifacts, and resulting in new artifacts.
Hexagon Agent is a contextual entity acting as a catalyst of a process, enabling, facilitating, controlling, or affecting its execution.
